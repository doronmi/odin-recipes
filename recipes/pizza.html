Pizza recipe 1

Description

This is a nice and easy pizza recipe.

Ingredients (for 4 small pizzetas)

  • 2 Pitta bread
  • 1 400g can of crushed tomatoes
  • 150g grated cheese

Preparation

  1. Cut pita breads across to create circles
  2. Spread the tomatoes
  3. Sprinkle the cheese
    Optional additions:
  • Sliced onions
  • Sliced mushrooms
  • Olives
